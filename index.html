Earth is portrayed as a planet in crisis. The movie is set in a near future where environmental conditions have drastically deteriorated. The world is experiencing widespread crop failures, leading to a global shortage of food. Additionally, massive dust storms plague the planet, making agricultural practices extremely challenging. These adverse conditions have severe consequences for human society. Governments are struggling to cope, and there's a sense of societal decline. The population is grappling with famine, resource scarcity, and a general breakdown of the social order. The situation appears bleak, and the focus is on the immediate survival of the human species in the face of environmental catastrophe.
